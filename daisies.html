This photo was taken at a diagonal angle. This helps to make the composition appear dynamic, and create visual interest. There is a feeling of motion as the viewer looks deeper into space in this image. This is bolstered by the many lines in this photo. Each of the stems in this garden is a line that helps contribute to visual interest. Chromatically speaking, this photo makes use of analogous colors. The green and yellow shades in this composition harmonize well with each other. There is also contrast between the bright, saturated yellow flowers, and the muted, desaturated green stems.

The concept of diminution plays is an important element in this photo. The composition becomes less focused and saturated the farther one looks into the background. This helps to create a sense of space and depth. This is a very textured composition, due to the many overlapping stems in the greenery. This composition expresses a feeling of vibrancy, representing the season of Spring. It was taken on an Iphone camera in New Jersey.
